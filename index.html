Values & Facilities

BGMI VALUES

BGMI offers you some professional courses – ✅ Apparel Merchandising (Knit, Woven & Sweater) ✅ Fashion Design & Development ✅ HR, Admin & Social Compliance ✅ IE – Planning, Lean Management & GSD ✅ CAD- Pattern & Marker Making ✅ GPQ & Quality Control Management ✅CLO – 3D Virtual Sampling Course ✅ In-House Training & Corporate Training and many more.

- INSTITUTE CODE – 50738

- AFFILIATED WITH – BANGLADESH TECHNICAL EDUCATION BOARD

BGMI (Bangladesh Garment Management Training Institute) was established in 2011 with the aim of producing highly competent human resources with requisite levels of technical proficiency for Bangladesh’s burgeoning garment industry. It is operated by experts having relevant educational backgrounds and professional experience in the industry.

OUR FACILITIES

Our goal is to train professionals in a most innovative & realistic way with the help of highly qualified expertise who are serving this sector from many years in various field of garment industry.
We are committed to providing you quality and innovative training for RMG sector. We can be your 1st choice for any professional courses those who are dedicated to build-up their career in this trade.
A group of young energetic people established BGMI with the aim to be the best professional institute within the country.
All are the founders of this institute are well established in their current position & dedicatedly working with a spirit to develop this institute with high international standard.
